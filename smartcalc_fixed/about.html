About SmartCalc

SmartCalc is a free, ad-supported collection of online calculators designed to help you make smarter financial and everyday decisions — quickly and accurately.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves access to reliable, easy-to-use tools for financial planning and daily calculations — without needing to download an app, create an account, or pay anything. SmartCalc is built to be fast, mobile-friendly, and always free.

What We Offer

SmartCalc currently offers 9 free calculators covering finance, health, and daily use:

  • EMI Calculator – Plan your loan repayments
  • SIP Calculator – Estimate mutual fund investment returns
  • Loan Calculator – Calculate total loan cost and repayment
  • GST Calculator – Add or remove GST from any amount
  • Percentage Calculator – Quick percent calculations
  • Discount Calculator – Find final price after sale discounts
  • BMI Calculator – Check your Body Mass Index
  • Age Calculator – Calculate exact age from date of birth
  • Date Calculator – Find the difference between any two dates

How We're Funded

SmartCalc is free to use. To keep the site running and cover hosting costs, we display advertisements via Google AdSense. These ads are clearly labeled and do not interfere with your ability to use any calculator.

Privacy & Data

All calculations happen entirely in your browser. We do not store the numbers you enter. Please see our Privacy Policy for full details.
