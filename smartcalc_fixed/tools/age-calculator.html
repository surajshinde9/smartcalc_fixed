How Age is Calculated

Age calculation is more nuanced than simply subtracting birth year from the current year. A precise age calculation must account for whether the birthday has occurred in the current year, handle different month lengths (28–31 days), and properly manage leap years.

Our age calculator uses a day-accurate method: it calculates the difference between your date of birth and today's date, accounting for all calendar irregularities. The result shows your age in years, months, and remaining days — the most complete way to express your exact age.

Beyond years and months, knowing your total days lived is a fascinating perspective. It helps you appreciate the passage of time and can be useful for legal, medical, and administrative purposes that require precise age verification.
